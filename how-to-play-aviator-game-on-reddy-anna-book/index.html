What is the Aviator Game?

Aviator, essentially, is a betting game wherein the bet is placed once and there is a wait for a plane to take off. What is important is the determination when the player intends to cash out — if the plane takes off before the player does this, the player will lose his or her stake. It’s safe to say that a game such as this is timed to the last second therefore making Aviator very thrilling in terms of gambling.
